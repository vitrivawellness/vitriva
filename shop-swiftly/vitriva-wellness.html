Why Magnesium Matters

"Magnesium is a critical intracellular mineral involved in over 300 enzymatic reactions, including ATP synthesis, neuromuscular signaling, and cardiovascular regulation."

Magnesium bisglycinate is absorbed via amino acid transport pathways, improving uptake and retention.

📚 Volpe SL, 2013, Advances in Nutrition – Magnesium plays a key role in muscle function.

Stress & Nervous System

📚 Boyle NB et al., 2017, Nutrients – Magnesium may reduce subjective anxiety.
